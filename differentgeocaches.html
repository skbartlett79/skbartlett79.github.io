DIFFERENT TYPES OF GEOCACHES

GEOCACHE CONTAINERS

Micro-caches - something smaller than a film canister such as a pill bottle or a container for contacts

Small Caches - size range of about a film canister to the size of the sandwich such as a small tupperware container

Regular caches - range in size from a sandwich to about the size of a shoebox such as a large tupperware container

Large caches - Can be anywhere in size of a shoebox to size of a large suitcase such as a large military ammo can

Other Size - This usually describes a container that is not typical such as a birdhouse or a hollowed out peice of wood
